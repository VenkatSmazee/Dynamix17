Dynamix 2K17
Dynamix’17 is a national level symposium for the engineering students to endorse their talents in various fields. Accepting defeat before even trying is the dreadful way to failure. Active participation with diligence, encouragement and focus are the main motto in organising the symposium. The platform is given here to exhibit your explicit talent and for acknowledgement.

Rules:

  • First 125 participants are allowed to participate in first come and first serve basis and only spot registration.
  • Registration closes by 9 am.
  • Candidates must carry their college ID card and Formal is mandatory.
  • Participation certificates will be given for those who clear prelims and may alter according to the event.
  • Paper & Abstract of Project should be sent through mail to dynamix17@saec.ac.in on or before 4th of August.
  • Confirmation mail for paper presentation and project display is mandatory.

      CADstar '17

        “There are three responses to a piece of design- yes, no and WOW!” WOW is the one to aim for. Creative designs are only expressed through their designer. The true designer is not one who is inspired, but one who inspire others. So here is an opportunity for you to use your designing skills to an effective extent.

        Rules:

      • Team participation is not allowed. One member per system.
      • Prelims consist of a design and written test on basic design knowledge.
      • Final round includes a part modelling and assembly.
      • Results will be declared based on dimensional accuracy and usage of commands in the model tree.
      • No extra time will be given at any circumstances.
